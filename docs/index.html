Every HDB block vs the nearest DBS/POSB ATM

On 25 Jun 2026 the Association of Banks in Singapore committed to an ATM, branch or NETS cashpoint within 500 m (straight-line) of every HDB block by end-2027. This map shows how far every HDB block is from the nearest DBS/POSB cash point today — one bank group's network, so a lower bound on industry coverage.

Method & caveats

  • What the commitment actually says: DBS, OCBC, UOB and NETS jointly — an ATM, bank branch or NETS cashpoint (cash-back at participating merchants) within 500 m of every HDB block by end-2027; near transport hubs, NEA hawker centres and major supermarkets (FairPrice, Sheng Siong, Cold Storage, Giant) by end-2026. Distance is straight-line, per the ABS release.
  • This map underestimates coverage: it shows DBS/POSB machines and branches only — no OCBC/UOB ATMs, no NETS cashpoints. A red block here may already be served by another bank.
  • Straight-line ≠ walking distance: 500 m as the crow flies can be a much longer walk (roads, canals, expressways).
  • HDB blocks: HDB Existing Building footprints (data.gov.sg), building centroids; includes all HDB buildings (some are carparks/commercial).
  • ATM/branch locations: DBS branch-locator public content API; "ATM" = cash-withdrawal machines (ATM, talking ATM, cash recycler). Deposit-only machines and AXS kiosks are excluded.
  • Cashpoints (toggle, off by default): NETS cash-back with a purchase at DBS/POSB Cash-Point merchants — 7-Eleven, Giant, Cold Storage, Jasons Deli (up to S$200) and Guardian (up to S$100), per posb.com.sg; Changi Airport outlets excluded, buzz kiosks omitted. Supermarket locations from NEA's licensed-supermarket register (data.gov.sg); 7-Eleven and Guardian outlets from © OpenStreetMap contributors (ODbL) — community-mapped, so a few outlets may be missing or stale.
